About Me

As I believe in the power of transformation, I've decided to make a career change after 25 years as a DJ and a small business owner. I’m finally pursuing my love for computer science. Now as a graduate of the Galvanize Full Stack Web Developer Program. My goal is to start a career in technology, learn as much as possible, and grow as a company grows.
